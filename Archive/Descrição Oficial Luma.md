Workshop: Obsidian x AI Agents (Obsidian Synapses) - A Protocol for Human and Machine Intelligence

The interfaces we inherited were designed to simulate an office inside a screen. Files in folders, documents on desktops. Now that machines are becoming genuine collaborators, this inherited structure matters more than it used to. The way you arrange your knowledge determines what AI can understand about you and how faithfully it can act on your behalf.

Obsidian Synapses is a methodology for building that arrangement with intention. In this hands-on session, led by Stéfano Mastella - acoustic engineer and writer of the Ipê News newsletter - you will work inside Obsidian as a personal knowledge environment where canvas maps, graph connections, and AI agents converge into a working system already tested daily across engineering projects, editorial production, and team coordination.

You will see a live workflow and build something during the session. You will leave with a clear sense of how personal knowledge becomes a communication layer - between you and your own thinking, between you and AI, and eventually between many minds building together.

That last thread connects directly to Ipê Mind Tree, the collective intelligence project from last season. The arc runs from your own vault outward, toward how communities share context and act on shared understanding.

No prior Obsidian experience required. Bring a laptop and curiosity.

Duration: approximately 2 hours | Language: English
